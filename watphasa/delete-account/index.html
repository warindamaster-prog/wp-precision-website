Delete Account and Data

Effective date: May 2026

You can delete your WatPhasa account inside the app from Account > Delete account. If you no longer have the app installed, use this page to request deletion.

Request Deletion by Email

To request deletion, email contact@wpprecisionsystems.co.uk using the email address linked to your WatPhasa account.

Please include the email address linked to your WatPhasa account so we can identify the correct account.

What Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is completed, we aim to delete the following app database records linked to your WatPhasa account:

  • Account profile.
  • Favorites.
  • Practice progress.
  • Practice scores.
  • Subscription status stored in the app database, if applicable.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited records required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or payment compliance. Purchase history managed by Google Play may be retained by Google according to Google Play policies.

Contact

For account or data deletion questions, contact WP Precision Systems at contact@wpprecisionsystems.co.uk.
