Manufacturing Quality Control

Inspection and production quality controls designed for repeatable manufacturing performance.

Manufacturing quality control depends on clear requirements, reliable measurement and quick feedback when variation appears. WP Precision Systems Ltd. helps define and improve the controls that keep production aligned with specification.

  • Inspection planning and control points
  • Measurement method review
  • Nonconformance and containment support
  • Quality data visibility and reporting
  • Production handover and training material

Focus

Turn inspection activity into usable production control, not isolated paperwork.

C.1 Quality Services

QMS, audit and calibration quality support for SMEs.

Quality services help SMEs build, audit and improve their Quality Management Systems, with particular focus on ISO 9001, ISO 17025 and calibration-related requirements.

Audit Support (ISO 9001/17025)

Delivery: 1-2 weeks

Best for: Manufacturers preparing for an external ISO audit, including initial certification, surveillance or recertification.

Scope

  • Pre-audit gap analysis
  • Documentation and evidence file review
  • Remediation plan
  • Mock audit session
  • Internal audit report

Calibration Audit Readiness

Delivery: 1-2 weeks

Best for: Manufacturers facing a customer audit or supplier qualification with calibration scrutiny.

Scope

  • Calibration certificate chain-of-traceability review
  • Asset register completeness check
  • Calibration schedule and overdue analysis
  • Vendor accreditation verification, UKAS / ILAC-MRA
  • Calibration-specific audit pack preparation

QC Assessment

Delivery: 1 week

Best for: SMEs wanting a QMS health check before pursuing or maintaining ISO 9001.

Scope

  • QMS gap analysis against ISO 9001
  • Control plan review and development
  • Inspection and measurement system review
  • Statistical Process Control, SPC, setup guidance
  • Non-conformance and CAPA process review
  • Operator inspection procedure training

Deliverables

  • QMS gap report, clause-by-clause
  • Updated control plans
  • CAPA template and workflow
  • Training summary

QC System Setup

Delivery: 2 weeks

Best for: SMEs that have grown beyond ad-hoc quality processes and need a formal QC system.

Scope

  • QC framework design
  • Inspection procedures, SOPs
  • Quality metrics dashboard
  • Documentation templates
  • Team training, 1 day
  • 30-day support after delivery

Calibration System Setup

Delivery: 2 weeks

Best for: Manufacturers establishing a formal calibration management system from scratch.

Scope

  • Asset register design and population
  • Calibration schedule and frequency rules
  • Vendor onboarding and SLA agreements
  • Calibration certificate template and storage structure
  • Internal calibration procedure, where applicable
  • Operator training, half-day
