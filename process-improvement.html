C.3 Process Services

Lean, Six Sigma, FMEA and 8D support for manufacturing performance.

Process services apply structured improvement methodologies to improve manufacturing process efficiency, reduce waste and increase yield.

FMEA + 8D Workshop

Delivery: 1-2 days on-site

Best for: Quick-win team training and live problem-solving. Strong foot in the door offer.

Scope

  • Pre-workshop scoping call
  • Day 1: FMEA training and facilitated FMEA on chosen product or process
  • Day 2 optional: 8D problem-solving methodology and live 8D on current issue
  • Templates and toolkit for ongoing use

Process Improvement

Delivery: 1 week

Best for: SMEs with inefficient or undocumented workflows.

Scope

  • Current-state process mapping using Value Stream Mapping
  • Waste and inefficiency identification
  • Root cause analysis using 5-Why and Fishbone
  • Future-state process design
  • SOP drafting and documentation, up to 3 SOPs
  • Prioritised implementation roadmap

Deliverables

  • Current and future state process maps
  • Gap analysis report
  • Prioritised action list with effort and impact matrix
  • Draft SOPs

Yield Improvement Investigation

Delivery: 2-3 weeks

Best for: Manufacturers with declining or stagnant yield. Scope and quotation confirmed after scoping. Strong fit for electronics and PCBA manufacturers.

Scope

  • Yield baseline measurement with weekly or monthly trend
  • Pareto analysis of failure modes
  • Root cause investigation for the top 3-5 failures
  • Corrective and preventive action plan, CAPA
  • Implementation support where in scope
  • Re-baseline after 30 days

Production Line Optimisation

Delivery: 1-2 weeks

Best for: Manufacturers needing to increase throughput or improve OEE.

Scope

  • OEE baseline measurement and analysis
  • Bottleneck and constraint identification
  • Line balancing assessment
  • Layout and flow optimisation review
  • Cycle time reduction analysis
  • Shift pattern and resource utilisation review

Deliverables

  • OEE baseline report
  • Bottleneck analysis with root causes
  • Improvement priority matrix with ROI estimates
  • Layout recommendations
  • Lean improvement plan

Manufacturing Transfer Support

Delivery: scope-dependent, typically 6-10 weeks

Best for: Operations moving production between sites. Scope and quotation confirmed after scoping.

Scope

  • Tester replication across receiving sites
  • Validation pack, IQ/OQ/PQ, for transferred testers
  • On-site knowledge transfer and operator training
  • Bilingual EN/TH support for UK companies with Thailand or SEA operations
  • Final transfer report and sign-off
