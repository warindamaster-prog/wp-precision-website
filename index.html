FAQ

Common questions, answered.

If your question isn’t here, a 30-minute discovery call is the fastest way to a real answer.

Do you only work with companies that need all three service lines?

No. Each service line stands alone. Many customers start with one — usually the calibration tracking software or a single engineering project — and add the others as the relationship grows.

What size of manufacturer is this designed for?

UK SME manufacturers in the 50–250 employee range, and electronics contract manufacturers of any size where calibration and test discipline matter to your customers’ audits.

Are you ISO 9001 / 17025 ready?

Yes. Retention windows, certificate handling, audit packs and process documentation are designed against ISO 9001 and ISO 17025 requirements — with UKAS / ILAC-MRA vendor verification on calibration work.

Can you support sites outside the UK?

Yes — particularly UK companies with Thailand or wider Southeast Asia operations. Bilingual EN / TH knowledge transfer is a standard option on manufacturing transfer projects.

How are engineering projects priced and contracted?

Fixed-price wherever possible. Custom builds are confirmed by a signed Statement of Work. Deliverables are owned by the client on full payment, in editable formats (Word / Excel / PDF as appropriate).

What if we already have a calibration provider?

External calibration vendor costs are charged at cost, with no markup. We coordinate the existing vendor relationship — we don’t replace it unless replacing is the right answer for you.
